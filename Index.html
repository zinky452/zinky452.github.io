About

Andrew is a British Film Director, currently based in Newcastle Upon Tyne. He is passionate about film and he has created many shorts which have been screened at film festivals world wide.

Although Directing is Andrew's forte, he has a keen interest in cinematography and has shot many of his own films.

Another big passion of Andrew's is music. Andrew has played in different bands since he was young age and has toured around the UK. Recently he has begun to score music for his own films.
